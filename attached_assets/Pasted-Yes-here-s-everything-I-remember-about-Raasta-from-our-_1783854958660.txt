# 🚍 Raasta — Community-Powered Transit Network

### Core Idea

**Raasta** is a **community-powered public transport navigation app** for Pakistan.

Unlike Google Maps, it **doesn't rely on official GPS trackers installed on buses**.

Its core philosophy is:

> **"We don't track the buses. We track the people."**

Instead of tracking vehicles, Raasta uses passengers' phones to determine where buses are, which routes are active, travel times, and fares.

---

# The Problem

Pakistan's public transport suffers from:

* No live bus tracking
* Unclear routes
* Unknown fares
* Difficult route planning
* No reliable arrival estimates
* Tourists and new commuters struggle

---

# The Solution

Raasta lets the **community build the transit network together.**

Every passenger who uses the app contributes anonymous transit data.

As more people use Raasta:

* routes become more accurate
* fares stay updated
* ETAs improve
* bus locations become more reliable

without requiring expensive GPS hardware.

---

# User Flow

### 1. User enters destination

Example:

> FAST University → Dolmen Mall

---

### 2. Raasta suggests multiple options

Example:

* 🟢 Saver
* 🔵 Express
* 🟡 Comfort

Each option shows:

* estimated time
* estimated fare
* transfers
* walking distance

---

### 3. User selects a route

Raasta guides the passenger to

* correct stop
* boarding point

---

### 4. Ride Detection

Once onboard,

the app detects movement using:

* phone GPS
* speed
* motion

No manual tracking required.

---

### 5. Ride Ends

When the passenger gets off,

Raasta asks

> "How much fare did you pay?"

This updates the community database.

---

### 6. Crowd Updates

Thousands of passengers continuously improve

* fares
* route accuracy
* stop locations
* travel times

---

# Community Model
